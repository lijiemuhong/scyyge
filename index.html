Sichuan Yiyuan General Equipment Co., Ltd. is located in High-Tech Industrial Zone, Ziyang City, Sichuan Province. Built upon Ziyang Citys foundation as a major chemical equipment production base during the Third Front Construction era, the company has developed into a professional manufacturer of natural gas compressors. After nearly 20 years of development, the company has received honors including National High-Tech Enterprise, National Technology-based SME, and Sichuan Province Specialized and New SME. The company maintains comprehensive ISO9001 quality, health and safety, and environmental management systems. Always pursuing the development philosophy of First-Class Products, Superior Service, Customer Satisfaction, our product quality has earned widespread recognition and trust from users.
