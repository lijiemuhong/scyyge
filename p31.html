Product Features---Compressor

Compressors for natural gas. shale gas. coalbed methane. hydrogen. and other gases

      ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

      The compressors designed and produced by Sichuan Yiyuan Company are based on the original design and production of high and medium pressure chemical compressors. introducing international advanced compressor concepts. exploring and innovating. and developing reciprocating piston type horizontal opposed balance D-type and horizontal opposed balanced M-type compressors with independent intellectual property rights. They have the characteristics of high speed. small size. reliable operation. energy saving and low noise.

              


 The compressors of Yiyuan Company mainly have the following characteristics:
1. In line with the international trend of high-speed compressor development. it has a wide range of applications and can be used in various occasions such as natural gas refueling stations. CNG stations and natural gas boosting stations. wellhead gas collection stations. pipeline transmission stations. and ultra-high pressure gas lift boosting.
2. It can adapt to the pressurization of hydrogen. nitrogen. natural gas and other media under micro pressure conditions.
3. The company's high-speed piston compressor (1500 revolutions per minute) occupies a smaller area than low-speed compressors. making installation and maintenance convenient and fast.

4. Advanced control. intelligent automatic control and protection. capable of outdoor all-weather unmanned operation.
5. The body adopts advanced 3D sand mold casting technology. which is more beautiful and durable.
6. Can work under environmental conditions of -51~50 ℃. The unit is fully air-cooled. without the need for cooling water or antifreeze. reducing operating costs and meeting environmental and energy-saving requirements.
7. Overall sled installation. easy to install. the whole machine can be installed without a foundation.
8. All pipeline valves. components. and vulnerable parts are from brands that have been verified for more than 10 years. ensuring the quality of the entire machine and extending its lifespan.


The high-quality machine heads or machines produced by the company have been widely used in domestic and foreign oil and gas station projects. with precise design parameters and operational reliability recognized by users.


         


   

Modular design of the entire machine. highly integrated main and auxiliary equipment. one click operation. PLC control. full process monitoring and operation. automatic shutdown protection.

It can achieve value-added functions such as variable frequency speed control of the main motor. full air cooling technology for system temperature reduction. installation and operation without prefabricated foundations. remote monitoring. and remote online services.

Comprehensive advantages of Sichuan Yiyuan compressor:
Save installation costs. civil engineering costs. and operational energy consumption. Reduce the difficulty of human-machine interaction. do not take up water resources. have simple and easy maintenance. guarantee 8000 hours of trouble free operation. and design a 20-year lifespan for the host.
